Graphics Processing Unit:

The graphics processing unit or also known as the graphics card is another important component. What is a graphics processing unit and why is it needed? It is a component that aids the central processing unit in processing graphical data for the computer. An individual would need this component for high-end gaming and applications that would require higher graphics.

Whether an individual is purchasing a pre-build, or they are buying a custom build or even building their own computer, the graphics processing unit is an important component to take into consideration. Graphics processing units come in many shapes and sizes and a person can have a dedicated graphics processing unit or one built onto the central processing unit. The two common manufacturers of graphics processing units are Nvidia and AMD.

Graphics processing units can range from 100 US dollars to over 1000 US dollars, depending on the price point and the power needed for what an individual wants to accomplish with their pre-build or self-built computer. It is thought that a lot of graphics were only used in high-end gaming, but there are other programs that need the high graphics. Some examples would be the graphics needed for machine learning and neural networks, video editing, and programs dealing with 3-D models such as AutoCAD.
