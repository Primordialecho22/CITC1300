Rock City

There are multiple mountians around Chattanooga, Tennessee including, Lookout mountian, Signal mountain, and
Sand mountain to name some. Rock City and Ruby Falls are two attractions that bring many guests to Lookout mountain.
At Rock City you can see seven different states when there are clear skys. The seven different states are Tennessee,
Kentucky, Virginia, South Carolina, North Carolina, Georgia, and Alabama.

Mountians come in all shapes and sizes, such as Stone Mountian in Georgia to Mount Everest in
Mahalangur Himal sub-range of the Himalayas. The height of Mount Everest is 8,848.86m (29,032 ft)
and the height of Stone Mountian is 1686 feet.
